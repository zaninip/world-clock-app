# Privacy Policy for World Clock App

**Last updated:** December 1, 2025

## Information We Collect

World Clock collects and uses your location data only to determine your current timezone. This data is:
- Used only locally on your device
- Never transmitted to external servers
- Never stored permanently
- Never shared with third parties

## How We Use Your Information

Your approximate location is used solely to:
- Detect your local timezone automatically
- Display the correct local time in the app

## Data Storage

- Favorite cities are stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences
- No personal data is collected, stored, or transmitted to external servers
- No analytics or tracking tools are used

## Third-Party Services

This app uses the GeoNames API to search for cities. When you search for a city, the search query is sent to GeoNames servers. Please refer to [GeoNames Privacy Policy](https://www.geonames.org/export/) for more information.

## Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
